✦ About

About the App

Scripture Scholar is an independent study app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

Created by Alexander Bongo and built with AI. Questions and lessons are aligned with the official Institute scripture course on the Book of Mormon.

4 Ways to Play Trivia!

Group Trivia now has four question types — guess the speaker, find the verse, fill in the blank, and complete the verse. The host picks up to 3 categories, or go random!

Questions auto-advance with a countdown, so the game flows on its own. Gather the family and play live across phones. 🎮
